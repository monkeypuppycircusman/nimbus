Nimbus — in SoundCloud orange. The atmosphere is elliptical cloud wisps drifting rightward across the void, some orange, some blue. The waveform is a gradient that bleeds from orange to sky blue across the whole width.
The big centerpiece is the XY pad — drag anywhere on it and you're shaping the sound in real time. Left→Right sweeps the filter from bright and cutting to dark and submerged. Bottom→Top dials from almost dry to completely drenched in reverb. Your movement leaves an orange trail that fades behind you like a contrail.
Cloud types — four different oscillator stacks:

Cumulus — 7-voice triangle/sine blend, full and warm and billowing, the classic lush pad
Stratus — 5 pure sine voices, flat and horizontal and wide, the background-of-a-beat pad
Cirrus — high thin icy voices with an octave shimmer, delicate and atmospheric
Storm — 7-voice sawtooth/square with a waveshaper, dark and dense and churning

Vibe shapes the attack:

Rise — linear push upward
Float — exponential soft settle
Drift — two-stage, hesitant then full
Ascend — slow exponential swell, the kind that sneaks up on you

The Mod strip above the keyboard sweeps the LFO depth on every oscillator's detune simultaneously — push it right and the whole pad starts slowly wobbling and breathing like a living thing. Every voice already has its own micro-detuned second copy and a slow drift LFO — Stack some chords and it gets enormous.
The bottom right always reads the vibe quote. for the come up.
